Good to Know

Questions, answered

Everything you need before your first appointment or spray tan.

How do I prep for my spray tan?
Shower, exfoliate, and shave at least 4 to 6 hours before your appointment. Come with clean skin, no lotion, deodorant, makeup, or perfume. Wear loose, dark clothing and bring flip-flops so you can leave glowing without smudging.
How long does a spray tan last?
Most tans last 7 to 10 days with proper care. Wait 8 to 24 hours before your first rinse, moisturize daily, avoid long hot soaks, and pat dry instead of rubbing. A hydration mist add-on helps it fade evenly.
What areas do you serve for mobile appointments?
Mobile spray tans are available across North Jersey, based in Madison and Chatham and covering surrounding Morris, Union, and Essex County towns. Reach out if you are nearby and not sure, group and bridal travel can usually be arranged.
What is the acne program and how long does it take?
It starts with a consultation to map your skin and triggers, then a custom home-care routine paired with bi-weekly treatments. Most clients see meaningful clearing within 3 to 4 months. It is a guided process, not a one-time facial.
What is your cancellation policy?
Please give at least 24 hours notice to reschedule or cancel. Late cancellations and no-shows may be subject to a fee. The full policy is shown when you book online through GlossGenius.
